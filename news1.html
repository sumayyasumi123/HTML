KERALA CM IN BAHRAIN

Date :17-Oct-2025

Bahrain:Kerala Chief Minister Pinarayi Vijayan is currently on an official visit to Bahrain, marking the beginning of his Gulf tour. He arrived in Bahrain on the night of October 15 and was warmly received by Indian Ambassador Vinod K. Jacob, prominent community leaders, and several officials. Today, October 17, CM Vijayan will inaugurate the Pravasi Malayali Sangamam at the Bahrain Keraleeya Samajam Diamond Jubilee Hall, scheduled for 6:30 pm. The event aims to strengthen cultural bonds and engage with the diaspora on developmental initiatives. Notable attendees include Minister Saji Cherian, Lulu Group Chairman M.A. Yusuffali, and representatives from Lok Kerala Sabha and Norka Roots. The Chief Minister's visit is part of a broader outreach to the Gulf region, highlighting Kerala's commitment to its global Malayali community and recognizing their vital contributions to the state's economy and cultural identity.
